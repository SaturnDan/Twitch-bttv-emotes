Privacy Policy

This privacy and security policy explains how Twitch BTTV Emotes handles your information when you use the app.

What data is stored

The only data that is stored or used is:

  • Collects stack traces and relevant application state when an application crashes.
  • Collects device and OS information to assist with debugging crashes.

The above data is shared with a third-party for crash detection and debugging purposes. No other data is collected, such as usage data or personal information about you.
